GHCR packages accumulate untagged version cruft (per-arch child manifests, provenance attestations, superseded cache digests) with no retention anywhere. Adds: scripts/ghcr-personal-cleanup.sh (human-run, dry-run-default, deletes whole orphaned personal-namespace packages in one call); .github/workflows/package-cleanup.yml (weekly untagged-only retention over all 12 org packages via dataaxiom/ghcr-cleanup-action, multi-arch aware, ships dry-run and arms via the PACKAGE_CLEANUP_DRY_RUN repo variable); and operations/package-hygiene.md documenting the mechanism, cleanup, and arming.
